Education

My primary education gave me a solid foundation on which my subsequent education was built.
My secondary educatioin is divided into two stages; The first three years was the junior secondary where I obtained Junior Secondary School Certificate.
This gave me the qualification to attent the Senior Secondary School that specialised in Science Subjects for another three years. There I obtained my Senior Secondary School Certificate.
Then, I was admitted to University of Ibadan to study BSc.Agricultural Extension and Rural Development. At the end of my degree programme, I worked for some years then proceeded to obtain my Masters degree in Management with Human Resource from University of Chester.
I practiced in field of Human Resource for several years then move on to train as a teacher in University of Bolton.
